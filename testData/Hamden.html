Parent/Guardian supervision is required for all children under 13 years old, and a reservation is required for both the parent/guardian and the child.　

Please make a reservation for yourself (i.e. the accompanying guardian) when reserving a space for a member that is under 13. Children under 13 will not be allowed entry without an accompanying parent/guardian, and without a reservation for the parent/guardian, we cannot guarantee access.

If the child is a member, the parent does not need a membership provided they are only there to supervise (membership not required to belay or spot only).
